About ScanIQ

Our Mission

ScanIQ is a specialized scanner designed to help you make informed decisions about food and dietary products for managing PCOS and hormonal health. By analyzing ingredient lists through advanced image recognition, we help you understand the potential impact of foods on your hormones and insulin levels.

Primary Focus: PCOS & PCOD

ScanIQ is primarily designed for analyzing food products and dietary ingredients that can impact hormonal balance, particularly for those managing:

  • PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome)
  • PCOD (Polycystic Ovary Disease)
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Insulin resistance

Food products are categorized into three risk levels:

  • Moderate Risk - Consume in moderation
  • PCOS Friendly - Low glycemic impact, hormone supportive
